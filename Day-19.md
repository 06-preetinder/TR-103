## 📝 Summary

Today's session focused on one of the fundamental concepts of Deep Learning—Backpropagation. I learned how data flows through a neural network during forward propagation, how prediction errors are calculated using a loss function, and how backpropagation computes gradients to update weights and biases. The session also explained the role of the chain rule and how optimization algorithms such as Adam use these gradients to improve model performance.

- Understood the workflow of neural network training.

- Understood how gradients are computed using the chain rule.
- Learned how weights and biases are updated during training.
